Camel Migration Expected Soon

Wild Bactrian camels typically live in herds of 6-20 members, although they can occasionally be solitary or in groups up to 30 individuals. In their native range, a population density of five camels per 100 square km has been estimated. Camels are not territorial animals. occasionally multiple camel herds will cross paths and form an assembly of camels, which can reach 500 individuals.
